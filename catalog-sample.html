Seven Generations of Tea Excellence

Nestled in the pristine highlands of Dosenbo at 500 meters elevation, our family has been cultivating exceptional Uji tea for seven generations. Our commitment to 100% organic, pesticide-free cultivation ensures the purest expression of Japanese tea tradition.

Each tea variety represents the unique terroir of the Uji region, where misty mornings and rich volcanic soil create the perfect conditions for premium tea cultivation.
